Media Composer

Isabel Costa Lopes

A Brazilian-American media composer with a deep love for storytelling through music. Isabel has scored several short films, worked for YouTube channels amassing over 4+ million views, and interned at Sparks & Shadows, the studio of Emmy-winning composer Bear McCreary. She is especially drawn to fantasy, period dramas, and anything with heart, and loves creating orchestral soundscapes that turn your ideas into life.
